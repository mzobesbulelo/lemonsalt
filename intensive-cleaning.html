About Us

LemonSalt offers to you our professional intensive cleaning services for both commercial and industrial work spaces. We take the headache out of training staff buying supplies, scheduling and do it all for you, so managing your business is your number one priority.

Our professional cleaners are well-trained by the SETA accredited Cleaning Africa (Pty) Ltd. They have an eye for detail and our managerial staff are good communicators, excellent schedulers and know how to serve you right.
