Company Summary

LemonSalt Cares for the youth in our community. The LemonSalt Education and Training Division is proud to present the Mental Health Awareness Program that was established with the objective of not only educating the public about mental health but also to reduce the stigma and discrimination that people with mental illness are often subjected to.

Company Summary

Our Services

Our sessions cover the following topics:

  • Teenage Depression
  • Suicide / Living with suicidal thoughts
  • Anxiety Disorder / High functioning anxiety
  • Coping with Bullying
  • Depression and school
  • Drugs and alcohol in school
  • Mental health in the workplace
  • Borderline personality disorder
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
